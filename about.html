About ClassBerg

ClassBerg provides JEE, NEET, and MHT-CET classes with a fast and simple student testing framework, virtual classrooms to share class material, and a private question database to ease and automate test creation.

Why

Why was ClassBerg created?

ClassBerg was created by a college student and aimed to be a student-first learning management system. It aimed to provide an elegant user-experience and appealed to the students, since they would be the primary consumers of the site.

ClassBerg went through many stages and pivots to become the software it is today. For example, initially, ClassBerg aimed to let students create and share their own practice tests. It also aimed to be a resource where students could find guidance regarding the decisions they would face after they pass their 12th standard - choosing their college and branch of study.

Later, we decided to focus our development on our learning management system, especially since there already existed excellent resources guiding students to choose the right college and branch of study for them.

Who

Hi, I'm Hrushikesh Vaidya, the developer of ClassBerg.

I'm an electronics engineering student at the College of Engineering, Pune. I developed ClassBerg after my own journey of giving the JEE and MHT-CET, and I wanted to create a platform that would involve its users, especially the students themselves, in its design process and grow while keeping these stakeholders at the center.
